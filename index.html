What Is Droom?


Droom is the future of job search and career placement. Leveraging technology and job placement experience seamlessly into one integrated platform where both job seekers and providers can maximize their time and increase the efficiency all in one swipe.


Job Seeker!

Droom allows job seekers to customize and create a profile detailing their work history and skillset. Once you create your profile, you can begin your search with looking for jobs with similar intrest. Upon a match, both parties can begin communicating.

Job Provider!

Droom allows job providers to create a profile and post job oppertunities. Once the profile is created, you can begin looking to find your preferred canididate. Upon a match, you will be able to communicate with the seeker about the job opportunity.

Features

Droom offers many key features both seekers and providers may take advangate of. The easy to use layout makes it effortless to begin the search for that perfect job, or even that perfect canididate with just a swipe.
